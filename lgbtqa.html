LGBTQA+ Mental Health

Trigger warning!

Statistics says that LGBTQIA+ are more four times as likely to attempt suicide than their peers.

Transgender are almost four times as likely as a cisgender to struggle with a mental healt disease.

LGBTQIA+ is not a mental illnes or disorder. However people that are in the LGBTQIA+ community many of them struggle with mental illness. That might be because they are at particular risk for experiencing discrimination, upsetting experience, panic and humiliation.

You are not alone!
